Armaggeddon Website Redesign

Unsolicited Redesign
Armaggeddon is a Singaporean brand that specializes in professional gaming gear and equipment that is functional and affordable. It was established in 2011. Armaggeddon product design emphasizes the look and feel of their products. The brand aims to provide high quality affordable gaming gear for gamers.
The client brand is pretty new to the industry and therefore gets overshadowed by the bigger companies like Razer and Logitech. They need a stronger brand identity that helps them appeal to more gamers not only in Singapore but in their secondary target demographics in Malaysia and Indonesia.
