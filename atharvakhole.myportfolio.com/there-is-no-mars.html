There is No Mars.

•The Project Brief is to produce an animation to go along with the audio sequence “How to be a good ancestor” by Roman Krznaric.
•The animation should not only provide a visual counterpart to the audio, but also illuminate and add to the content through its visual storytelling.
Objectives:
•To tell a story through the animation that will enrich the contents of the audio file.
•To have captivating visuals to grab and maintain the attentions of online audiences.
•To make witty use of visuals and attention to detail in order to bring a new audience to the subject matter and to RSA as a whole.
The Process:
•Visual Inspiration and storyboarding
Illustrating Assets:
Rigging and Animation
Final Deliverables:

1. Animated short video
2. Video Game Concept (There is No Mars.)
There is No Mars is a Windows/Mac video game. The concept of the game revolves around managing a small colony of humans towards sustainability. The name of the game highlights how there is no backup option/ a second planet for us to move to once we destroy this one. Put your management skills to test as you avoid Climate Crisis in this 2D simulation game.
